ABOUT ADIDAS

Adidas ( German : adidas ) is a German -based sportswear company , a brand consisting of several companies, adidas, Reebok sportswear, Taylormade golf products ( sold by Adidas in 2017 ) , and Rockport ) for athletic shoes, (sold by Adidas in 2015 ) to New Balance . Besides sports shoes, the company produces other products such as bags, T-shirts, watches, eyeglasses and other sports-related apparel. Adidas ranks first in the sportswear industry in Europe, and ranks second in the world after its American competitor, Nike . The company's designs of clothing and footwear feature three parallel stripes, the same as the official logo nowadays. In 2008, the company's revenues amounted to 10.799 billion euros , after it was 10.299 billion euros in 2007, or about 15.6 billion US dollars . Its market value in 2018 reached $14.3 billion, and it owns 1,200 factories around the world, and the company employs more than 775,000 workers in 65 countries. Adidas sponsors and enters into agreements with famous clubs in the world of football , such as Real Madrid , Juventus , Bayern Munich and Manchester United. And the teams of Germany , Spain and Brazil , in addition to the most important football players such asLionel Messi , Mesut Ozil , Sergio Oguero and Paul Pogba . After returning from World War I , Adolf ("Adi") Dassler began manufacturing his sports shoes in his kitchen in Herzogenaurach, Bavaria . In 1924 , he was joined by his brother, Rudolf (Rudi) Dassler, and together they founded the Dassler Brothers' Shoe Factory. At the 1928 Olympics , Dassler equipped many athletes with the goal of expanding the company's activity on an international level. During the 1936 Summer Olympics in Berlin , Dassler was the shoe maker for gold medal winning quadruple Jesse Owens of the United States. And at the end of World War II , the plant was transformed for the production of anti-tank weapons. In 1947, the brothers separated and Rudy founded Puma , and Adi founded Adidas. The company was officially registered as adidas AG (000 in lowercase), on August 18, 1949. The term Every Day I Dream About Sports , though sometimes considered adidas as the original name, is used retroactively. The word adidas is made up of "Ade" (a pseudonym for Adolf) .
